The complete brand, UI, and design token reference for Inkra — formerly Scrybe. Everything your team needs to build, market, and scale.

01 — Brand Strategy

Inkra turns conversations into structured work automatically.

So teams can focus on the work instead of documenting it. Inkra captures what matters — from VoIP calls, standups, user research sessions, patient visits, and client intake — and routes it into forms, tasks, reports, and compliance records without manual effort.

Category

Conversation-to-Work Platform

Not a transcription tool. Not a meeting bot. Not a CRM. Inkra is the AI layer that sits inside conversations and automates the work that follows.

Core Idea

Work shouldn't disappear into conversations.

Teams spend hours talking, explaining, discussing, reporting. The real work happens after. Inkra bridges that gap automatically.

Audience

Mid-market → Enterprise → Government

Starting with nonprofits. Expanding to healthcare, legal, sales, UX research, and engineering — any industry where conversations drive outcomes.

Brand Personality

Protective Intelligent Warm Institutional Trustworthy

Emotional Reaction

When someone lands on Inkra, they should feel:

Safe Calm Serious Confident

Never: excited, overstimulated, confused, skeptical.

Voice & Tone

Marketing (Human)

"Less documenting. More doing."

Notion-style clarity. Short sentences. Calm. Never hype AI — sell the outcome.

Product (Institutional)

"3 tasks created from your last call."

Precise. Functional. No personality where accuracy matters. System messages state facts.

What Inkra Never Does

Uses "AI-powered" as a headline. The technology is invisible — sell the outcome.

Uses neon gradients, robot imagery, glowing particles, or anything that screams "AI startup."

Makes the UI feel busy. Inkra products feel like a quiet office, not a mission control dashboard.

Uses exclamation marks in product copy. Calm confidence, not enthusiasm.

03 — Typography

Tiempos Headline + Söhne

Two fonts, two jobs. Tiempos Headline carries warmth and authority in headings — it reads as institutional without being stiff. Söhne handles body text, UI labels, and product interfaces with quiet precision. Both are from Klim Type Foundry, designed to pair together.

Licensing note: Tiempos Headline and Söhne require licenses from klim.co.nz. Until purchased, the design system uses Lora (Tiempos stand-in) and DM Sans (Söhne stand-in) via Google Fonts. Swap two CSS variables when ready — no other changes needed.

07 — Voice Visualization

Ink Listening — Rorschach Blobs

Inkra's most distinctive element. Instead of horizontal waveforms or equalizer bars, voice is visualized as organic ink blobs that morph with speech — like Rorschach ink blots responding to sound. They're slightly symmetrical along a vertical axis, never perfectly mirrored. Soft, organic, imperfect edges. This is what makes Inkra visually unmistakable.

Recording State — Active

Organic ink blobs expand, merge, and separate with speech. Slightly symmetrical like a Rorschach test — never mechanical bars.

Idle / Silence State

Small calm blobs. Minimal movement. Still alive — the system is listening.

Behavior Rules

Shape

Organic, rounded edges. Slight asymmetry. Blobs merge and separate. Mirrored along vertical axis — Rorschach symmetry, but not identical left-right.

Motion

Ink moves slowly even when sound changes quickly. Sound spikes produce smooth shape transitions (200–500ms). Never jittery or mechanical.

Color

Ink Blue primary at 70–85% opacity. Multiple overlapping blobs create depth through transparency. Silent state drops to 40–50% opacity.

Compact Indicator

Recording · 4:32
Recording active

Where Voice Blobs Appear

Recording screen

Primary use. Large centered blobs. The main visual focus during active calls.

Meeting join

Smaller version. Shows system is listening. Subtle ambient motion.

Processing state

Blobs slow down and settle as transcription completes. Ink "drying" metaphor.

09 — Marketing Patterns

Ink Textures — Marketing Only

These patterns exist in marketing materials, social media, and ad content — never inside the product UI. They make Inkra instantly recognizable in a LinkedIn feed or pitch deck. The product stays clean.

Stippling

Ink dots. Best for: hero backgrounds, social post textures. Opacity 8–12%.

Cross-Hatching

Diagonal ink lines. Best for: section backgrounds, card textures. Opacity 5–8%.

Cross-Hatch (Double)

Two-direction hatching. Best for: emphasis areas, feature highlights. Opacity 4–6%.

Mixed Dots

Layered dot sizes. Best for: announcement posts, event graphics. Opacity 8–10%.

10 — Logo System

Voice → Ink

The Inkra mark is a single continuous line that starts as a sound wave and transitions into a confident pen stroke. Voice becoming ink. Conversation becoming work. The stroke has real ink pressure — thickening at the wave crests, tapering at the ends like a nib lifting off paper.

Ink Black — monochrome version

Ink Blue — primary brand version

Inkra

Symbol + Wordmark lockup

Mark Anatomy

Left entry: Thin, organic — the voice arriving. Low amplitude, like someone starting to speak.

Center peaks: Stroke thickens at crests — ink pressure increases. The conversation is happening. Variable, not uniform.

Right exit: Wave calms, stroke gains weight and confidence, then tapers like a pen lifting off paper. Voice has become ink. Work is done.

Usage Rules

Minimum size: 24px height for digital, 12mm for print.

Clear space: 1x the mark height on all sides.

Colors: Ink Black on light backgrounds, white on dark backgrounds, Ink Blue for brand emphasis.

Never: Add gradients, glow effects, drop shadows, outlines, or place on busy backgrounds. The mark should breathe.

12 — Loading States

Doodle Loading — Phase 2

A future brand layer. During loading screens (recording initializing, AI processing, generating reports), the UI shows small sketch-like doodle animations — as if someone is drawing while on the phone. This humanizes wait times and reinforces the conversation-to-work metaphor.

Style Rules

Stroke: 1–1.5px, Ink at 30–50% opacity

Motion: Lines draw slowly (600–1200ms). Feels like someone drawing.

Content: Quick note scribbles, small arrows, circles, underlines, tiny diagrams.

Never: Cartoonish, playful illustrations, thick lines, bright colors.

Where Doodles Appear

Recording initializing, AI processing, generating reports, joining meetings, syncing records. Not in core product chrome — only in transitional moments.

Status: Documented for Phase 2. Not required for MVP.
