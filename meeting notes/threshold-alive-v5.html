The Model

Apply. Build. Ship. Compound.

Apply

Membership is curated. We look for builders who ship — not talkers who plan. Show us what you've made or what you're making.

Build

Access shared Stripe, legal entities, development tools, and a tight network of people who actually give real feedback. No more building alone.

Ship

Launch under the Syndicate umbrella. Payments, compliance, and operations are already wired up. You build the product — we built the rails.

Compound

Products that hit traction spin out. You keep majority equity. The syndicate retains a stake. Every win adds to the portfolio — and every member's upside grows.

Infrastructure

Shared Stripe, legal entities, and dev tooling. No overhead of doing it yourself. Start building on day one.

Flexibility

Pursue your own venture on your own schedule. Full-time, part-time, nights and weekends — you decide.

Ownership

Clear path to spin out when the time is right. Majority equity stays with the builder. Always.
