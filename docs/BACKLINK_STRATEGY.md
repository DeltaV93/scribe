## Content Assets for Outreach

### For Guest Posts
Create these assets to support outreach:

1. **Author Bio** (50 words)
   > [Founder Name] is the founder of Inkra, a conversation-to-work platform that turns phone calls, meetings, and conversations into documentation, reports, and tasks automatically. Previously [background]. Based in [location].

2. **Company Boilerplate** (100 words)
   > Inkra is a conversation-to-work platform for organizations drowning in documentation. Instead of typing the same information into multiple systems, teams talk—and Inkra generates case notes, intake forms, compliance reports, and follow-up tasks automatically. Serving nonprofits, healthcare organizations, sales teams, and professional services, Inkra reduces documentation time by 60% while improving data accuracy. Currently in pilot with select organizations. Learn more at inkra.ai.

3. **Topic Ideas for Pitches**
   - "The Hidden Cost of Documentation: Why Your Best Employees Are Drowning in Admin"
   - "From 16 Hours to 6: How Conversation AI is Changing Nonprofit Case Management"
   - "Why 'Just Take Notes' Doesn't Work: The Case for Ambient Documentation"
   - "The Future of CRM: When Your Conversations Update Your Pipeline"
   - "WIOA Compliance Without the Headache: Automating Grant Reporting"

### For Press/PR
1. **Press Release Template** - Draft for pilot launch
2. **Founder Story** - Background, why this problem, personal connection
3. **Data Points** - "16 hours/week on documentation", "5x data entry", "60% time savings"

## Monthly Rhythm

### Week 1: Outreach
- Send 10 guest post pitches
- Submit to 3 directories
- Respond to HARO queries

### Week 2: Follow-up
- Follow up on pitches (7 days after)
- Draft accepted content
- Monitor new backlinks

### Week 3: Content
- Publish 1 blog post (internal)
- Create 1 linkable asset (infographic, report, tool)
- Engage on social (amplify partners)

### Week 4: Analysis
- Check new backlinks in Ahrefs/Moz
- Update tracking sheet
- Plan next month's targets

## Linkable Assets to Create

High-value content that earns backlinks:

1. **"State of Documentation" Report**
   - Survey 100+ professionals on documentation time
   - Create shareable statistics
   - Pitch to publications

2. **ROI Calculator**
   - Interactive tool: "How much is documentation costing you?"
   - Embeddable, linkable

3. **Industry Benchmarks**
   - "Average documentation time by industry"
   - Infographic format

4. **Templates/Resources**
   - SOAP note templates
   - WIOA reporting checklist
   - Client intake form examples

## Don'ts

- Don't buy links (Google penalty risk)
- Don't use link farms or PBNs
- Don't over-optimize anchor text
- Don't spam directories with duplicate content
- Don't ignore nofollow links (still valuable for traffic)
